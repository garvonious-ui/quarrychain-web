QuarrySwap is a non-custodial AMM decentralized exchange built natively on QuarryChain. It's designed for speed, simplicity, and real yield.

## How It Works

Swap any QRC-20 token pair instantly with a 0.3% fee — 0.25% goes directly to liquidity providers. No order books, no intermediaries, no custody risk. Connect your QuarryWallet, pick your pair, and trade.

## Liquidity Pools

Deposit equal parts of any token pair to become a liquidity provider. You receive QLP tokens representing your share of the pool. Every swap that touches your pool earns you a proportional cut of the fees.

## Triple Yield Stacking

QuarrySwap introduces three stacking yield sources. Protocol usage fees from every swap. Network token rewards for staked QLP positions. And lending interest rates driven by leverage demand. Stack all three for compounding returns.
