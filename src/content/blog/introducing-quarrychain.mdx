QuarryChain is a Layer 1 blockchain network built from the ground up on Delegated Proof of Stake consensus. We designed it to solve the problems that hold existing chains back: slow throughput, high fees, energy waste, and governance that favors insiders over communities.

## What We Built

QuarryChain processes 100,000 transactions per second with 3-second finality. The network runs on 27 community-elected Quarry Miners who produce blocks in rotating rounds. No expensive mining hardware. No capital-heavy staking minimums. Just democratic governance backed by real performance.

The QuarryChain Virtual Machine (QVM) is fully EVM-compatible. If you write Solidity today, your contracts deploy on QuarryChain without modification. Web3.js, Ethers.js, MetaMask — your entire toolchain works natively.

## The Ecosystem

We're building more than a chain. QuarrySwap brings non-custodial trading with AMM liquidity pools and yield farming. QuarryWallet gives users full self-custody over QRY, QRC-10, and QRC-20 tokens. QuarryScan provides transparent block exploration for every transaction and contract on the network.

## What's Next

The testnet is live. The seed round is open at $0.25 per QRY token. Mainnet launch, public ICO, and the full ecosystem rollout are on the roadmap.
